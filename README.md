# The Wheel of Taste 

Spin the wheel and discover your next meal!

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Features

- Cuisine Selection: Choose from 20 different cuisines including Italian, Indian, Japanese, American, etc.
- Budget Control: Filter suggestions by price range: under $15, $15-$30, or over $30
- Interactive Wheel: Smooth spinning animations with haptic feedback
- 300+ Meal Options: Diverse suggestions tailored to your preferences
- Polished UI: Gradient backgrounds, fluid transitions, and animations
